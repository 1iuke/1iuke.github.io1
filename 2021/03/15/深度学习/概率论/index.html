sum rule

p(X)=∑Yp(X,Y)p(X) = \sum_{Y}{}p(X,Y) p(X)=Y∑​p(X,Y)

product rule

p(X,Y)=p(Y∣X)p(X)p(X,Y) = p(Y|X)p(X) p(X,Y)=p(Y∣X)p(X)

对称性

p(X,Y)=p(Y,X)p(X,Y) = p(Y,X) p(X,Y)=p(Y,X)

Bayes’ theorem

p(Y∣X)=p(X,Y)p(X)=p(X∣Y)p(X)p(X)=p(X∣Y)p(X)∑Yp(X,Y)=p(X∣Y)p(X)∑Yp(X∣Y)p(Y)p(Y|X) = \frac{p(X,Y)}{p(X)}\\ =\frac{p(X|Y)p(X)}{p(X)}\\ =\frac{p(X|Y)p(X)}{\sum_{Y}{}p(X,Y)}\\ =\frac{p(X|Y)p(X)}{\sum_{Y}{}p(X|Y)p(Y)} p(Y∣X)=p(X)p(X,Y)​=p(X)p(X∣Y)p(X)​=∑Y​p(X,Y)p(X∣Y)p(X)​=∑Y​p(X∣Y)p(Y)p(X∣Y)p(X)​

贝叶斯定理的分母p(X)=∑Yp(X∣Y)p(Y)p(X)=\sum_{Y}{}p(X|Y)p(Y)p(X)=∑Y​p(X∣Y)p(Y)看做归⼀化常数，⽤来确保公式（4）左侧的条件概率对于所有的Y 的取值之和为1。
