关于概率论的一些公式

1 sum rule

p(X)=∑Yp(X,Y)p(X) = \sum_{Y}{}p(X,Y) p(X)=Y∑​p(X,Y)

2 product rule

p(X,Y)=p(Y∣X)p(X)p(X,Y) = p(Y|X)p(X) p(X,Y)=p(Y∣X)p(X)

3 对称性

p(X,Y)=p(Y,X)p(X,Y) = p(Y,X) p(X,Y)=p(Y,X)

4 Bayes’ theorem

p(Y∣X)=p(X,Y)p(X)=p(X∣Y)p(X)p(X)=p(X∣Y)p(X)∑Yp(X,Y)=p(X∣Y)p(X)∑Yp(X∣Y)p(Y)p(Y|X) = \frac{p(X,Y)}{p(X)}\\ =\frac{p(X|Y)p(X)}{p(X)}\\ =\frac{p(X|Y)p(X)}{\sum_{Y}{}p(X,Y)}\\ =\frac{p(X|Y)p(X)}{\sum_{Y}{}p(X|Y)p(Y)} p(Y∣X)=p(X)p(X,Y)​=p(X)p(X∣Y)p(X)​=∑Y​p(X,Y)p(X∣Y)p(X)​=∑Y​p(X∣Y)p(Y)p(X∣Y)p(X)​

贝叶斯定理的分母p(X)=∑Yp(X∣Y)p(Y)p(X)=\sum_{Y}{}p(X|Y)p(Y)p(X)=∑Y​p(X∣Y)p(Y)看做归⼀化常数，⽤来确保公式（4）左侧的条件概率对于所有的Y 的取值之和为1。

5 概率密度probability density

p(x∈(a,b))=∫abp(x)dxp(x)≤0∫−∞∞p(x)dx=1p(x\in(a,b)) = \int_{a}^{b}p(x) dx\\ p(x) \leq 0\\ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}p(x)dx = 1 p(x∈(a,b))=∫ab​p(x)dxp(x)≤0∫−∞∞​p(x)dx=1

概率密度–>概率–>累积分布概率密度–>概率–>累积分布概率密度–>概率–>累积分布

考虑 变换x=g(y)x=g(y)x=g(y) 区间(x,x+δx)(x,x+\delta_x)(x,x+δx​) 变为 (y,y+δy)(y,y+\delta_y)(y,y+δy​) 则有px(x)δx⋍py(y)δyp_{x}{}(x) \delta_x \backsimeq p_{y}{}(y) \delta_ypx​(x)δx​⋍py​(y)δy​

py(y)=px(x)∣dxdy∣=px(g(y))∣g′(y)∣p_y(y) = p_x(x)|\frac{dx}{dy}| = p_x(g(y))|g'(y)| py​(y)=px​(x)∣dydx​∣=px​(g(y))∣g′(y)∣

结论： 概率密度最⼤值的概念取决于变量的选择

P(z)=∫−∞zp(x)dxP(z) = \int_{-\infty}^{z} p(x)dx P(z)=∫−∞z​p(x)dx

满足 P′(x)=p(x)P'(x)=p(x)P′(x)=p(x)

8 贝叶斯概率

后验⋍\backsimeq⋍似然x先验
